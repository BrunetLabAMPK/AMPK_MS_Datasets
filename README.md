# AMPK_MS_Datasets
Peak-matched Protein Prospector mass spectrometry files generated for "(“Identification of AMPK phosphorylation sites reveals a network of proteins involved in cell invasion and facilitates large-scale substrate prediction” (Schaffer et al, 2015).

This document contains descriptions of all the peak-matched Protein Prospector output mass spectrometry files associated with Schaffer et al, 2015 (“Identification of AMPK phosphorylation sites reveals a network of proteins involved in cell invasion and facilitates large-scale substrate prediction”); these files are available here for download. Files are grouped by biological experiment. Within each biological experiment, files are grouped according to technical mass spectrometry runs. See Schaffer et al, 2015 for further description of each assay.


Assay A:

Files are grouped by technical mass spectrometry replicates. “untn” or “un” = 293T cells with AMPK beta 1 and gamma 1 over-expressed. “wt” or “WT” = 293T cells with AMPK alpha 2 wild type, beta 1, and gamma 1 over-expressed. “as” or “AS” = AMPK alpha 2 analog specific, beta 1, and gamma 1 over-expressed. “40 minus” refers to the labeling time (number) and whether 2DG was added to the labeling buffer (minus or plus).
